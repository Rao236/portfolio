Professional Profile

I am an undergraduate Computer Applications student with a strong academic and practical orientation toward artificial intelligence, secure systems, and low-level software engineering. My technical foundation spans machine learning, systems programming, and embedded platforms, with a growing focus on building reliable, resource-efficient intelligent systems.

I have completed formal training in full-stack web development and maintain hands-on experience across Python, C/C++, and Java, complemented by practical exposure to Linux-based development environments and version-controlled collaborative workflows. My project work reflects an interest in system-level design, including the development of a lightweight operating system prototype on microcontroller hardware and the implementation of an on-device knowledge-centric AI system leveraging semantic retrieval and retrieval-augmented generation techniques.

In parallel with my individual technical work, I initiated and maintain an open-source technology community "CONECTOUT" aimed at collaborative learning and the development of practical, research-oriented software systems. Through this initiative, I focus on creating structured learning resources, encouraging reproducible engineering practices, and fostering contributions in areas such as artificial intelligence, systems programming, and secure computing.

Alongside applied development, I maintain a research-oriented mindset with exploratory work in cognitive-inspired decision modeling and conceptual investigations into quantum-resilient cryptographic methods. My long-term objective is to pursue advanced research and engineering in intelligent systems, with the goal of contributing to robust, secure, and scalable AI solutions that are deployable in real-world, resource-constrained environments.
